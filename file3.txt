Space Exploration Technologies Corp. (SpaceX) is an American spacecraft manufacturer, launcher,

 and a satellite communications corporation headquartered in Hawthorne, California. It was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk 

with the stated goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

 The company manufactures the Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy, and Starship launch vehicles, several rocket engines, Cargo Dragon and 

Crew Dragon spacecraft, and Starlink communications satellites.
